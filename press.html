Short Description

Yorokobi Studio (喜悦) is an independent visual novel studio creating emotional, character-driven stories about hardship, tenderness, and hope.

Long Description

Yorokobi Studio is a small, independent studio devoted to emotional visual novels. The name comes from the Japanese word yorokobi — joy — though the studio is interested in a specific kind of joy: the quiet light that is earned only after loss, fear, confusion, or loneliness.

Our work favours mood, character, and the small emotional turns that make a scene linger. We build slowly and openly, sharing development notes as projects take shape. The studio is currently in its earliest phase, focused on prototypes and its first public release, with a longer emotional project planned for the future.

Tagline

Joy earned through overcoming hardship.
