Support

Yorokobi Studio intends to keep its first projects accessible whenever possible. Future support will help transform small prototypes into polished visual novels — without ever rushing the work or losing its sincerity.

A Gentle Path

Where future support will go

Our first goal is to build with modesty and clarity. When the time is right, optional support may help us commission professional work while keeping early releases free or accessible. Every contribution would go directly into the craft:

  • Art direction and illustration
  • Original music and sound design
  • Translation into more languages
  • Editing and proofreading
  • Freelance support for specialised needs
  • Publishing, distribution, and ongoing maintenance
  • Future updates and new versions
